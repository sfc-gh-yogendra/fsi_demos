## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Investment Policy Statement establishes investment objectives, guidelines, and constraints for the {{PORTFOLIO_NAME}}. The IPS provides:
- Clear definition of investment objectives aligned with conservative risk profile
- Strategic asset allocation emphasising capital preservation
- Investment guidelines ensuring appropriate diversification and quality
- Performance evaluation framework with suitable benchmarks
- Governance structure with defined roles and responsibilities

This policy governs all investment activities and shall be reviewed annually.

---

## 2. Investment Objectives

**Primary Objective**: Capital preservation with modest income generation and minimal volatility. The portfolio seeks stable returns whilst protecting principal from significant loss.

**Return Target**: 4-5% annualised return over full market cycle (5-7 years), acknowledging returns will vary annually. This target aims to modestly exceed inflation whilst maintaining conservative risk profile.

**Risk Objective**: Minimise volatility and drawdown risk. Target maximum annual volatility of 6-9% and peak-to-trough drawdown not exceeding 12% during market stress. Conservative risk tolerance reflects capital preservation priority.

**Time Horizon**: Medium-term horizon of 5-7 years allows for modest equity exposure whilst maintaining predominant fixed income allocation for stability.

## 4. Investment Guidelines

**Permitted Securities**:
- Investment grade corporate and government bonds (BBB-/Baa3 or higher)
- Large-cap equities (market cap > $10B)
- Dividend-paying equities with established track records
- High-quality bond mutual funds and ETFs
- REIT funds for real estate exposure

**Quality Requirements**:
- Fixed Income: Minimum 85% Investment Grade (BBB-/Baa3 or higher)
- Maximum 15% below Investment Grade, no securities below B3/B-
- Equities: Established, profitable companies with dividend history preferred
- Avoid speculative or early-stage companies

**Concentration Limits**:
- Maximum 5% in any single issuer
- Early warning at 4% triggers review
- Maximum 20% in any single sector
- Maximum 50% in any single country
- Fixed income: maximum 3% in any single corporate issuer

**Prohibited Investments**:
- Individual securities below Investment Grade
- Leveraged or inverse ETFs
- Options, futures, derivatives (except for hedging with approval)
- Private placements or illiquid securities
- Commodities or commodity futures
- Short selling
- Securities on restricted lists

**Liquidity Requirements**: Minimum 95% of portfolio in securities tradeable within 5 business days.
