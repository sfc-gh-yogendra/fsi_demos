## Delivery vs Payment (DVP) Requirements
All equity settlements must be conducted on a Delivery vs Payment (DVP) basis unless otherwise approved by the Operations Committee. Free deliveries require dual authorization from Operations and Compliance.

## Settlement Failures Protocol
Notify counterparty T+1, escalate T+3, buy-in T+5

## Corporate Actions Processing
All splits, dividends processed automatically via custodian feed

## Tax Withholding
